Like being high in protein, being able to prevent brain depression, preventing porous bones, good for the heart, rich in selenium (an essential mineral for the body), maintaining the body's metabolic system, helping to maintain eye health, and many others. Chicken meat is one of the main ingredients for cooking. Chicken can be processed into delicious and special dishes. Chicken can be processed into a variety of appetizing menus, such as fried or baked. However, if you are bored with the same chicken dishes, you can make chicken into another special menu. But before starting to cook, you need to know how to cook chicken properly. First, choose a young chicken, young chicken meat has a more delicious taste and the texture of the meat is not too hard. Second, don't keep the chicken in the fridge too long. At least two days max. If it's too long, it can damage the freshness of the chicken and the texture can change. Third, when boiling chicken, use sugarcane juice or coconut water. Boiling with these two waters can make the chicken meat more tender and soft. Besides that, it can also add to the taste. But if you don't have the two waters, you can also boil the chicken as usual. Pay attention to the level of maturity and tenderness of the chicken. Can be full, healthy is also achieved.
